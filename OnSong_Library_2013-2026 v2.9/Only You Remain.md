Here are the basic chords I've heard from listening to the song:

Note: This song is split into different keys.  The verses are in the key of
 F and the Chorus/Bridge are in the key of D.  What makes this song 
more confusing is the fact that the artist sings the verses in sort of a 
harmony (listen to the song and you'll know what I mean).

Capo is put on the first fret.

(Can be alternately played with capo on second fret and using E and C# chord forms but is much harder on the fretting hand.)
