THE ROUGH SIDE OF THE SIDE OF THE MOUNTAIN (I'M COMIN' UP)
Rev. F.C. Barnes
Key: [C]

VERSE 1:
Oh Lord, I'm striving
Trying to make it through this barren land. 
But as I go from day to day 
I can hear my savior say,
"Trust me child, let me hold to your hand."

CHORUS:
I'm comin' up 
On the rough side of the mountain 
Holding to God, His powerful hand 
I'm comin' up on the rough side
And I'm doing the best to make it in.

VERSE 2:
I'm comin' up, Lord,
Athough my burdens sometimes 
They press me down.
But if I can only keep this faith 
I'll have strength just to run this race.
I'm looking for my starry crown.

VERSE 3:
This old race Will soon be over,
And there'll be no more race for me to run.
I've got to stand before God's throne.
All my heart aches will be gone.
I'll hear my Savior say, "Welcome home!"
