I'm So Glad He Found Me
Tempo: 65

Keywords: OS SOUTHERN GOSPEL SLOW

MEDLEY: HE CAME LOOKING FOR ME 

VERSE 1:
I was on the mountain
Wandering from the fountain
When I heard my Saviour speak to me
He said Come to me relenting
Of your sins repenting
And I will lead you out where you can see

CHORUS:
And I'm so glad He found me
with love surround me ( in love he bound me)
Put his arms around me
Then He led me to the shelter
And now, I'm one of His own
And oh, the joy of knowing
With my heart a-glowing
Someday I'm going
To my home, my home in glory
And walk on streets paved with gold

VERSE 2:
I will love Him ever
Stray from Him no never
He's the dearest friend I've ever known 
And when I see him  yonder
My love will then grow fonder
In that happy land (home) beyond the blue
