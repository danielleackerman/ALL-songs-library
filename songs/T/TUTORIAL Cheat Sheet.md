Suspensions:

To find a suspension in root; raise the 3rd 1/2 step.

Diminished:

To find a diminished in root; lower the 3rd and 5th 1/2step.

Augmented:

To find a augmented in root; raise the 5th 1/2 step.
(#5 is the same as aug. as in C7#5. is C7aug.

Half Diminished:

play the m7th and lower the 5th 1/2 step.
